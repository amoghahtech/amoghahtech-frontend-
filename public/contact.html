FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build a website?
A static website takes 5–10 working days. A WordPress site takes 2–4 weeks. Custom software and apps depend on complexity — we'll give you a clear timeline in our first meeting.
Do you offer milestone-based payment?
Yes! We offer flexible payment plans — usually 40% advance, 40% on development completion, and 20% on final delivery. EMI and custom plans are available for large projects.
Will I be able to update my website myself?
Absolutely. WordPress sites come with a user-friendly dashboard. Custom websites include an admin panel so you can update text, images and content without any coding knowledge.
Do you provide hosting and domain registration?
Yes. We set up domain registration, web hosting, SSL certificate, business email, and everything needed to go live — as a complete package.
What after-sales support do you provide?
All projects include 1–3 months free support after delivery. We also offer Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC) for ongoing support, updates and priority issue resolution.
Do you work with clients outside Bhopal?
Yes! We work with clients across all of India — fully remotely. We use video calls, WhatsApp, and project management tools to keep everything smooth regardless of location.
Can I get a free quote before committing?
Absolutely — free consultation and quote is always our first step. There is zero obligation. Just fill the form above or WhatsApp us and we'll respond within 24 hours.
